Who We Are Our Staff

The Yeshiva was founded with the help of Rabbi Hanoch Teller and Rabbi Dr. Howard Apfel, and is under the guidance of Rabbi Shlomo Kassin, Rabbi Yehoshua Feffer, and Rabbi Dovid Bagno Shlita.

All Rabbis and teachers are certified and professionally trained in their area of instruction.

Rabbi Baruch Teller
Menahel

Rabbi Teller received Semicha from the Israel Rabbinate and an MA in education from the prestigious Herzog Teachers College. He has taught for close to ten years in various elementary and high schools and post high school Yeshivot throughout the United States and Israel. He has taught a wide variety of subjects ranging from Gemara, Tanach, mussar, english and art.

Rabbi Adam Rappaport
Rebbe

Rabbi Rappaport completed his teacher training in the Achia Teachers College and has been successfully teaching for the last 22 years. He is currently furthering his educational skills at Michlalah College. His ability to forge a warm bond with each individual student is a great asset to Simchat HaTorah.

Rabbi Yehuda Diamant
Rebbe
